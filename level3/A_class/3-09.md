::: tip 语法

- I'm **too tired to** cook tonight.

使用 "形容词 + enough + to" , 表示“足够所以能”

- There's a sushi place that's **close enough to** walk to called Osaka.

- Oh yeah, that's **quite a popular** restaurant.

:::
