## 词汇课: 工作邀约

If you want someone to work for you, you give them a **job offer**.

If you **accept** a company's offer, it means you agree to work for them.

My company sends me a **check** every month to pay me for my work.

**A start date** is the date for the start of an important activity.

We should follow everything written in the **contract**.

It is important to **sign** names on the contract.

He is on **vacation** now. Let's not bother him with work.

I am tired of work. I really want to take some **time off**.

It is important to take a vacation from work every once in a while.

You have time off when you leave work for a short time for any reason.

如果你想让人为你工作，你要给他们一份工作邀约。  
如果你接受公司的邀约，就意味着你同意为他们工作。  
我的公司每个月都会给我发支票支付我的工作报酬。  
入职日期是指重要活动开始的日期。  
我们应该遵循合同中写明的一切内容。  
在合同上签名很重要。  
他现在正在休假，我们别用工作打扰他。  
我工作累了，真的很想休息一段时间。  
偶尔从工作中休假是很重要的。  
当你因任何原因短暂离开工作岗位时，你就有了休息时间。


## 语法课: 祈使句加条件短语的用法

```txt
Hey, thanks for watching my place.

Here's what you need to do.

If Cookie's water gets low, fill it up.

You can give him extra food if he looks sad.

If you want, have some of the chocolate in the kitchen.

Or, if you prefer, you can open a bottle of wine.

Also, eat the food in the fridge if you like.

If you need anything else, just text me.

Oh, and if Cookie gets sick, please call the vet.

That's all. Thanks again!

嗨，谢谢你帮我看家。这是你需要做的事情：如果Cookie的水变少了，请给它加满。
如果它看起来很难过，你可以多给它一些食物。如果你想的话，可以吃厨房里的巧克力。
或者，如果你更喜欢的话，可以开一瓶酒。另外，如果你喜欢，冰箱里的食物也可以吃。
如果你还需要其他任何东西，随时给我发短信。噢，还有如果Cookie生病了，请打电话给兽医。
就这些。再次感谢！
```

::: tip 语法

if 条件从句(一般现在时) +  主句(祈使句)

- **If Cookie's water gets low**, **fill it up**.

委婉表达:if 条件从句 + 主句(祈使句)

- **If you want**, have some of the chocolate in the kitchen.

:::

## 听力课: 签工作合同

```txt
Congratulations! You got a job offer.
Before you sign the offer letter, don't forget to do one thing.
That is to make sure it's the right job for you.

First, look at the "Job Title".
Make sure this is the job you want to do.
For example, your last job title was "Writer".
Your new job title is "Writing Manager".
Do you want to be a manager?
If not, talk to your new boss before you sign the offer.

Next, look at where the office is.
Is the office close to or far from your home?
Think carefully about a job that is far away.
It's hard to travel a long time to and from work.
Also, ask if you can ever work from home!

Last, the offer letter shows your working hours.
Do you need to work any nights or weekends?
Don't say yes to working hours that aren't good for you.
Does the offer still seem good? Great!
Good luck in your new job!


恭喜！你获得了工作录用通知。在签署录用通知书之前，别忘了做一件事：
那就是确保这份工作适合你。首先，查看"职位名称"。
确保这是你想做的工作。例如，你之前的职位是"作家"，而新职位是"写作经理"。
你想成为经理吗？如果不想，请在签署录用通知前与新老板谈谈。
其次，看看办公地点在哪里。办公室离你家近还是远？仔细考虑离家远的工作。
长时间通勤会很辛苦。另外，问问是否可以远程办公！最后，录用通知书会写明你的工作时间。
需要加班或周末工作吗？不要接受不适合你的工作时间安排。
这个录用通知看起来还不错？太好了！祝你在新工作中好运！
```
