**Material** is the information or ideas used to write something such as a book.

The author is collecting material for his new book.

A **genius** is a smart person who has unusual natural ability in a subject or activity.

Einstein was a genius.

My sister has a genius for leadership.

The **main** thing is the most important part of something.

The main reason I accepted this job is that I get to travel a lot.

**Cabbages** with a dark color are good for your health.

**Shrimp** is easy to cook, low in fat, high in **protein**,and tastes delicious.

Material（材料） 是用来写书等作品的信息或想法。
作者正在为他的新书收集材料。  
Genius（天才） 是在某个学科或活动中拥有非凡自然能力的聪明人。  
爱因斯坦是个天才。  
我妹妹有领导才能。
主要的东西是某事物最重要的部分。  
我接受这份工作的主要原因是能经常出差旅行。  
颜色较深的卷心菜对健康有益。  
虾容易烹饪，低脂肪，高蛋白质，而且味道鲜美。

This book talks about the top musical **geniuses** of all time.

The main goal of this meeting is to discuss the new project.

这本书讲述了有史以来最顶尖的音乐天才。  
这次会议的主要目的是讨论新项目。

## 语法课 : 表示数量的代词作为主语和宾语的用法

```txt
Thanks for awarding me Three-Star Chef of the Year!

I couldn't have won this award without my sensei, Yasu.

About 30 years ago, there were many Japanese restaurants in our neighborhood,
but only a few hired Japanese chefs.

Yasu was one of those.

I was lucky to learn from him.

He was such a great chef that you won't find another like him in the world.

"Treat your sushi like your baby."

It's what he kept telling me when I was his assistant.

I have kept all of this in my mind throughout my career.

谢谢您授予我年度三星厨师奖！
如果没有我的老师Yasu，我不可能获得这个奖项。
大约30年前，在我们附近有很多日本料理店，
但只有少数几家聘请了日本厨师。Yasu就是其中之一。我很幸运能拜他为师。
他是如此出色的厨师，世界上再也找不到像他这样的人了。
"把你的寿司当作自己的孩子一样对待。"
这是我在他手下当助手时，他一直对我说的话。
在我的职业生涯中，我一直铭记着这些话。
```

::: tip 语法回顾

数量限定词:

- a few people 一些人
- both parents 父母双方
- another drink 另一杯饮料

当它们作为**代词**时，可以独立作为主语和宾语使用，指代“一些”，“两个”和“另一个”。比如:

- Many people want to be rich, but only **a few** can.
- Do you want fish or beef? I want **both**.
- This cap is too small for me. Please show me **another**.

---

- this shirt 这件衬衫
- that banana 那根香蕉
- these houses 这些房子
- those tigers 那些老虎

它们也可以作为代词，表示“这个”、“那个”、“这些”、“那些”。

- one + of + these/those 某一个
- some+ of + these/those 某一些

- The apples are good! Do you want to try **one of these**?

---

当我们想要表达“所有的这个”或者“所有的那个”，可以使用 **all of this** 或 **all of that**，指代一个整体的事物。

The cake is huge. I can't finish **all of this**.

My mom gave me $50. I'm going to spend **all of that**.
:::

## 词汇课 : 餐饮服务

I'd like to book catering for our end of year party.

**Catering** is the business of providing food and drinks at events.

The hotel can host parties , weddings, and business events, and also offers catering services.

Some guests may not eat animal products. So we'dike to include some vegan options.

A vegan does not eat or wear anything that comes from animals, such as eggs, milk, or leather.

She is a strict vegan and doesn't use silk or leather.

我想为我们年终聚会预订餐饮服务。  
Catering（餐饮服务）是指在各种活动中提供食物和饮料的业务。  
这家酒店可以举办派对、婚礼和商务活动，也提供餐饮服务。  
有些客人可能不吃动物产品。所以我们想要包括一些纯素选项。  
严格素食主义者不吃也不穿任何来自动物的东西，比如鸡蛋、牛奶或皮革。  
她是一个严格的素食主义者，不使用丝绸或皮革。

Some guests have an allergy to milk. Do you have dairy-free options?

An **allergy** is a **medical condition** that makes you sick because you have eaten or touched certain things.

I have an allergy to animal hair.

I go vegan because I am allergic to meat and milk.

A **dairy-free** product doesn't contain milk.

The boy is allergic to milk, so he has **stuck to** a dairy-free diet.

有些客人对牛奶过敏。你们有不含乳制品的选择吗？    
过敏是一种医学状况，会使你因为吃了或接触了某些东西而生病。    
我对动物毛发过敏。   
我吃素是因为对肉类和牛奶过敏。   
不含乳制品的产品不含牛奶。   
这个男孩对牛奶过敏，所以他坚持无乳制品饮食。   

Can you provide me with a customized menu?

To **customize** means to make or change something to meet buyers' needs.

The car's color has been customized to the client's requirement.

Do I need to pay a deposit to reserve the catering?

When you pay a **deposit**, you pay the first part of the payment for something.

Tourists must pay a deposit to book the tour.

We put down a deposit on the apartment.

你能为我提供定制菜单吗？ 
定制意味着制作或改变某物以满足买家的需求。    
这辆车的颜色已根据客户的要求进行了定制。    
预订餐饮服务需要付定金吗？    
当你支付定金时，你支付的是某项服务或商品的第一部分款项。    
游客必须支付定金才能预订旅游行程。   
我们在公寓上支付了定金。 

::: tip 语法

- put down a deposit (on sth.) 付定金

- pay a deposit (for sth.) 付定金

:::
