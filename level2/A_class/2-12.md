::: tip 语法

主语(以 s 结尾的复数名词) + s' + 名词

- Those are the tour **guides' desks**.

所有格'用于陈述句及否定句

- That is my **manager's desk**.

:::

## 语法课: 不可数名词的用法

```txt
What do you want to do this weekend?

Shall we get a slice of pizza or a cup of coffee?

Sounds great, but I also want to go outside and enjoy the sunshine.

OK, Let's check the weather forecast.
Rain, rain, and more rain. The weather is not looking good.

Oh wait. I have an exam next Monday. I just remembered.
I'll just stay at home, listen to music and prepare for it.


这个周末你想要做什么？
我们去吃一块披萨或者喝杯咖啡怎么样？
听起来不错，但我也想去外面享受阳光。
好吧，我们看一下天气预报吧。下雨、下雨，还是下雨。天气看起来不太好。
哦等等，我下周一有一场考试。我刚刚才想起来。那我还是待在家里，听音乐并准备考试吧。
```

:::tip 语法

表示容器的词 + 不可数名词

- Shall we get a slice of pizza or **a cup of** coffee?

the + 不可数名词

- Sounds great, but I also want to go outside and enjoy **the sunshine**.

不可数名词

- I'll just stay at home, listen to **music** and prepare for it.

:::

## 听力课: 入职第一天

```txt
It's the morning before you start at your new job.
You're not feeling very confident.
Here is an idea of what the first day is like at a new job.
Plus some tips to help you feel less nervous on your first day.

First, you will go to Human Resources and show your ID.
You can ask the HR person any questions you have.

Next, you will meet your manager, and she will show you to your desk.
Your manager will introduce you to your co-workers.
Try to remember your co-workers' names.
This will help you build good relationships.

Next, your manager will give you a tour of the office.
You'll see the bathrooms, kitchens, meeting rooms, and break areas.
If you don't know the neighborhood,
ask your manager where most people eat lunch near the office.

Finally, after the tour, you can set up your desk and computer.
Good luck on your first day!


Plus some tips : 加上一些提示
set up your desk : 摆好你的桌子
set up your computer : 配置你的电脑


这是一份新工作开始的前一天早上。
你现在感觉不太自信。
以下是你在新公司第一天的大致情况，
另外也有一些建议可以帮助你在第一天不那么紧张。

首先，你会去人力资源部并出示你的身份证件。
你可以向HR人员询问任何你有的问题。
接下来，你会见到你的主管，她会带你去你的办公桌。
你的主管会把你介绍给你的同事们。
尽量记住同事们的名字，
这将帮助你建立良好的人际关系。

接着，你的主管会带你参观办公室。
你会看到洗手间、厨房、会议室和休息区。
如果你对附近不熟悉，可以问问主管大多数人平时在哪里吃午饭。
最后，在参观结束后，你可以布置你的办公桌并设置好电脑。
祝你第一天顺利！
```

## 口语课: 校园参观

```txt
Hey, Christina! Nice to see you!

Hi, Steve. I'm happy to be here.

Let me give you a tour of the school. Are you ready?

Yes! I'm excited.

This is the school's playground. We have many sports clubs.

Students must really love the playground.

Those are the classrooms. There is a television in each classroom.

Cool!

Let me show you the teachers' room next. Here's my desk.
This is my laptop. I keep my students' files in these folders.

Well, thanks for the tour. Oh, here... this is a calendar for you.
You can put it on your desk.

Thanks! You are so sweet!


嘿，Christina！很高兴见到你！
你好，Steve。我很高兴能来这里。
让我带你参观一下学校。准备好了吗？
是的！我非常兴奋。
这是学校的操场，我们有很多体育俱乐部。
学生们一定很喜欢这个操场。
那边的是教室，每间教室都有一台电视。
酷！
接下来我带你看看老师的休息室。这是我的办公桌。
这是我的笔记本电脑，我把学生的文件保存在这些文件夹里。
嗯，谢谢你的参观。哦，给，这是一份日历，送给你。你可以把它放在你的桌子上。
谢谢！你好贴心！
```

::: tip 语法

主语(以 s 结尾的复数名词) + s' + 名词

- Let me show you the **teachers'** room next....I keep my **students'** files in these folders.

所有格 s 用于陈述句及否定句

- This is the **school's** playground.

:::
